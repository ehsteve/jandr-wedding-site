About Us

Getting here

Fly into Geneva Airport (GVA). Take the train from the airport to Lausanne (Gare).

To Do - In Lausanne

Cathedral of Lausanne

Visit this medieval cathedral consecrated in 1275.

Olympic Museum

Visit the newly renovated Olympic museum.

Ouchy

Take a stroll along the lake in Ouchy and visit some Roman ruins.

Sauvabelin Tower

Visit the forest and climb its tower to get a panorama of the city.

Pédal'Eau on the Lake

Enjoy a ride on the lake on a pedal boat.

The Vivarium

Check out the largest collection of venomous reptiles in Europe.

The Mudac

Check out some modern art.

Un Musée Pour La Photographie

If you like photography...

To Do - In the area

Lavaux Vineyards

Go wine-tasting in a UNESCO World Heritage site

Chillion Castle

Visit a 1000 year old castle built on the Lake.

Caverns of Vallorbe

Head underground to see cascades, lakes, stalagtites and stalagmites.

Tulip Festival

Check out the Tulip Festival on the shores of the lake in beautiful Morges

Chocolate Train

Take to Chocolate Train to visit the Cailler Chocolate Factory.
